Pool Rules

  1. Pick 4 golfers. Total aggregate odds must meet or exceed 22,500.
  2. Lowest aggregate score (sum of all 4 golfers’ scores to par) wins.
  3. If a golfer misses the cut, you receive 10 strokes above the highest 72-hole score for that golfer.
  4. Payouts: 1st, 2nd, 3rd. Last year: $1,250 / $700 / $50.
  5. $50 entry fee. Entries must be submitted before first tee time Thursday.
  6. Email your 4 golfers and aggregate odds total to enter.
