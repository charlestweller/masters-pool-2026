Mulligan Pool Rules

  1. Pick any 3 golfers who made the cut — no odds requirement.
  2. Scoring is Rounds 3 & 4 only. Rounds 1 and 2 have no effect on standings.
  3. Lowest aggregate score (sum of all 3 golfers’ Round 3+4 scores to par) wins.
  4. Winner take all — $25 entry fee.
  5. Entries submitted Friday night after the cut.
